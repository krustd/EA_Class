# Seminar 2 Presentation Speech — Luckin Coffee 案例分析讲解稿

> 中英文双语对照，一次一段。建议以中文讲解为主，英文作为补充参考。

## 1. 开场：小组与企业选择 | Opening: Group and Enterprise Selection

**中文：**
老师好，我们是第四组。今天我们选择瑞幸咖啡（Luckin Coffee）作为案例企业，来展示平衡计分卡和商业模式画布的分析结果。选择瑞幸的原因主要有三个：第一，它的商业模式兼具实体门店运营、移动端数字化交易、供应链管理和数据驱动的客户运营，非常适合做企业架构分析；第二，瑞幸是一家中国本土企业，我们对它的业务场景更容易理解；第三，瑞幸经历过财务危机后的重建和快速增长，这个过程中战略目标与运营执行的匹配关系非常值得研究。

**English:**
Hello, teacher. We are Group 4. Today we selected Luckin Coffee as our case enterprise to present our Balanced Scorecard and Business Model Canvas analysis. There are three main reasons for this choice. First, Luckin's business model combines physical store operations, mobile-first digital transactions, supply chain management, and data-driven customer operations — making it highly suitable for enterprise architecture analysis. Second, Luckin is a Chinese company, so we can better understand its business context. Third, Luckin went through a financial crisis, restructured, and then achieved rapid growth — the relationship between strategic goals and operational execution during this process is very worth studying.

## 2. 企业概况：为什么是新零售模式 | Company Snapshot: Why "New Retail"

**中文：**
在开始分析之前，我们先明确了瑞幸的核心战略定位。瑞幸跟传统的星巴克模式不一样，它不走"第三空间"的路线，而是强调"买得起、买得到、买得快"。我们在报告中画了一个战略逻辑图，把瑞幸的模式定义为四个支柱：移动端优先的数字化下单、集成的供应链、密集的提货门店网络、以及数据驱动的运营。这四个支柱共同支撑起瑞幸的"新零售"定位。我们讨论的时候认为，理解这个底层逻辑很重要，因为它会贯穿后面平衡计分卡和商业模式画布的所有内容。

**English:**
Before diving into the analysis, we first clarified Luckin's core strategic positioning. Luckin differs from the traditional Starbucks model — it doesn't pursue the "third place" concept. Instead, it emphasizes affordability, accessibility, and speed. We drew a strategic logic diagram in the report, defining Luckin's model through four pillars: mobile-first ordering, integrated supply chain, dense pickup store network, and data-driven operations. These four pillars together support Luckin's "new retail" positioning. During our discussion, we agreed that understanding this underlying logic is critical, because it runs through everything in the Balanced Scorecard and Business Model Canvas that follows.

## 3. 平衡计分卡总览：四个视角的关系 | BSC Overview: How the Four Perspectives Connect

**中文：**
接下来是平衡计分卡部分。我们在正式开始填表之前，先讨论了四个视角之间的逻辑关系。我们认为这四个视角是层层支撑的：学习与成长视角提供人才、数据和创新能力，这些能力支撑内部流程的高效运转；内部流程的标准化和数字化又支撑了客户体验的提升；客户满意度和复购率的提升最终转化为财务绩效。所以我们先画了战略地图（Strategy Map），把这个从下到上的因果关系可视化出来，然后再开始填具体的内容。这样做的好处是，后面写 Objectives 和 Measures 的时候，每一层的目标都能和相邻层对应上。

**English:**
Next is the Balanced Scorecard section. Before filling in the table, we first discussed the logical relationships among the four perspectives. We believe these four perspectives support each other layer by layer: Learning and Growth provides talent, data, and innovation capabilities, which enable efficient internal processes; standardized and digitized internal processes then support better customer experience; improved customer satisfaction and repeat purchase rates ultimately translate into financial performance. So we first drew a Strategy Map to visualize this bottom-up causal chain, and only then filled in the specific content. The benefit of this approach is that when we wrote the Objectives and Measures, each layer's goals naturally aligned with the adjacent layers.

## 4. BSC — 财务视角 | BSC — Financial Perspective

**中文：**
财务视角的 Mission，我们强调的是"在保持价格可负担的前提下，实现可规模化的盈利增长"。这个定位很重要，因为瑞幸的核心竞争力之一就是性价比。如果为了盈利而大幅提价，反而会伤害它的市场定位。基于这个 Mission，我们设了三个 Objectives：第一是收入增长，通过门店扩张、提高购买频率和包装咖啡销售来驱动；第二是门店层面的盈利能力，通过平衡自营店的质量控制和联营店的轻资产扩张来实现；第三是成本管控，涉及原材料、租金、人工和营销支出。对应的 Measures 我们选了总收入增长率、营业利润率、同店销售增长、平均订单价值，以及不同渠道的收入拆分。这些指标合在一起，既能看规模增长，也能看盈利质量。

**English:**
For the Financial perspective, our Mission emphasizes "achieving scalable and profitable growth while maintaining affordability for mass-market customers." This positioning is important because affordability is one of Luckin's core competitive advantages. If they raised prices significantly just to boost margins, it would actually damage their market positioning. Based on this Mission, we set three Objectives: first, revenue growth driven by store expansion, higher purchase frequency, and packaged coffee sales; second, store-level profitability by balancing quality control in self-operated stores with asset-light expansion through partnership stores; third, cost discipline covering raw materials, rent, labor, and marketing. For Measures, we chose total revenue growth rate, operating margin, same-store sales growth, average order value, and revenue split by channel. Together, these indicators capture both scale growth and profit quality.

## 5. BSC — 客户视角 | BSC — Customer Perspective

**中文：**
客户视角的 Mission 是"让咖啡成为城市消费者方便、可负担、日常化的一部分"。这个表述直接对应瑞幸的"咖啡日常化"战略。我们设了三个 Objectives：第一是提高客户留存和复购频率，通过数字化会员体系和精准营销来实现；第二是提升便利性感知，包括快速取餐、可靠配送和高密度门店覆盖；第三是强化品牌信任，通过稳定的口感、品质、卫生和服务一致性来建立。我们讨论时特别提到，对瑞幸来说，便利性可能比口味本身更重要——因为它的核心客户群是上班族和通勤者，他们最在意的是"快"和"近"。对应的 Measures 包括月均交易客户数、复购率、客户终身价值、平均取餐时间、订单取消率和净推荐值（NPS）。

**English:**
The Customer perspective Mission is "to make coffee a convenient, affordable, and habitual part of everyday life for urban consumers." This directly reflects Luckin's "coffee as daily routine" strategy. We set three Objectives: first, increase customer retention and repeat purchase frequency through digital membership and targeted promotions; second, improve perceived convenience through fast pickup, reliable delivery, and dense store coverage; third, strengthen brand trust through consistent taste, quality, hygiene, and service. During our discussion, we specifically noted that for Luckin, convenience may matter more than taste itself — because the core customers are office workers and commuters who care most about speed and proximity. The corresponding Measures include monthly transacting customers, repeat purchase rate, customer lifetime value, average pickup time, order cancellation rate, and Net Promoter Score.

## 6. BSC — 内部流程视角 | BSC — Internal Process Perspective

**中文：**
内部流程视角的 Mission 是"运营一条紧密整合的从数字化到实体交付的价值链"。这个表述的核心词是"整合"——瑞幸的各个环节不是孤立的，而是通过数据系统串在一起的。我们设了三个 Objectives：第一，在自营店和联营店中标准化采购、生产、交付和质量控制的流程；第二，用数据系统来改善需求预测、库存补货和排班调度；第三，减少从 App 下单、支付、订单分配到售后反馈各个环节中的运营摩擦。这里我们在讨论时花了不少时间，因为内部流程对老师和同学来说可能不太容易直观感知。所以我们举了具体例子：比如瑞幸的后台系统能根据天气、历史和周边活动来预测某个门店的销量，然后自动调整原料配送和人员排班——这就是"数据驱动的内部流程"。对应的 Measures 包括库存周转率、缺货率、原料损耗率、订单准确率、系统正常运行时间和交易成功率。

**English:**
The Internal Process perspective Mission is "to operate a tightly integrated digital-to-physical value chain from demand forecasting to store fulfillment." The key word here is "integrated" — Luckin's processes are not isolated; they are connected through data systems. We set three Objectives: first, standardize procurement, production, fulfillment, and quality control across both self-operated and partnership stores; second, use data systems to improve demand forecasting, inventory replenishment, and labor scheduling; third, reduce operational friction in app ordering, payment, order routing, and after-sales feedback. We spent quite some time discussing this part because internal processes may not be intuitively visible. So we gave concrete examples: for instance, Luckin's backend system can predict a store's sales volume based on weather, history, and nearby events, then automatically adjust ingredient distribution and staff scheduling — that is what "data-driven internal processes" means. The corresponding Measures include inventory turnover, stockout rate, raw material wastage, order accuracy, system uptime, and transaction success rate.

## 7. BSC — 学习与成长视角 | BSC — Learning & Growth Perspective

**中文：**
学习与成长视角是四个视角中最"底层"的，它支撑着上面三层的能力。我们的 Mission 是"培养支撑快速创新、数字化运营和可持续扩张的组织能力"。三个 Objectives 分别是：第一，提升员工培训、标准操作流程合规性和店长管理能力——因为瑞幸有大量门店，每一家店的操作一致性直接影响客户体验；第二，利用销售数据、消费者反馈和区域口味偏好来加速产品创新——瑞幸每年推出大量新品，这个创新能力是它的核心竞争壁垒之一；第三，强化数据治理、分析能力和技术平台的韧性——因为瑞幸本质上是一家"技术驱动的零售公司"，数据和技术不是辅助工具而是核心基础设施。对应 Measures 包括培训完成率、人均产出、员工流失率、新品数量和新品成功率、数据准确率、仪表盘采纳率，以及 IT 事故解决时间。

**English:**
The Learning and Growth perspective is the "foundation" among the four — it supports the capabilities of the three layers above. Our Mission is "to develop organizational capabilities that support rapid innovation, digital operations, and sustainable expansion." The three Objectives are: first, improve employee training, SOP compliance, and store manager capability — because Luckin has thousands of stores, and operational consistency at every store directly impacts customer experience; second, accelerate product innovation using sales data, consumer feedback, and regional taste preferences — Luckin launches many new products each year, and this innovation capability is a core competitive moat; third, strengthen data governance, analytics capability, and technology platform resilience — because Luckin is fundamentally a "technology-driven retail company," where data and technology are not support tools but core infrastructure. The corresponding Measures include training completion rate, employee productivity, turnover rate, new product count and success rate, data accuracy, dashboard adoption rate, and IT incident resolution time.

## 8. BSC 的指标设计逻辑 | The Logic Behind Our BSC Indicator Design

**中文：**
关于 Objectives 和 Measures 的设计，我们特别注意了老师课上强调的一点——Objectives 是"你想实现什么"，Measures 是"你怎么知道实现了没有"，两者必须有逻辑对应关系。举个例子，我们在客户视角有一个 Objective 是"提升便利性感知"，那么对应的 Measures 就不能只写"客户满意度"，而是要具体到"平均取餐时间、配送完成时间、订单取消率"这些能直接反映便利性的指标。另外，我们还确保四个视角之间存在交叉验证：比如内部流程的"App 交易成功率"如果下降，就会影响客户的"订单取消率"，进而影响财务的"总收入增长率"。这种跨视角的因果关系正是平衡计分卡的核心理念——不要只看财务数字，要看到驱动财务数字的先行指标。

**English:**
Regarding the design of Objectives and Measures, we paid special attention to what the teacher emphasized in class — Objectives are "what you want to achieve," and Measures are "how you know whether you achieved it." The two must have a logical correspondence. For example, under the Customer perspective, we have an Objective to "improve perceived convenience." The corresponding Measures shouldn't just be "customer satisfaction" — they need to be specific indicators that directly reflect convenience, such as "average pickup time, delivery completion time, and order cancellation rate." Additionally, we ensured cross-perspective validation: for instance, if the Internal Process measure "app transaction success rate" drops, it will affect the Customer measure "order cancellation rate," which then affects the Financial measure "total revenue growth rate." This cross-perspective causal relationship is the core idea of the Balanced Scorecard — don't just look at financial numbers; look at the leading indicators that drive them.

## 9. 商业模式画布总览 | Business Model Canvas Overview

**中文：**
接下来是商业模式画布部分。我们的思路是先确定价值主张，再向外展开其他模块。瑞幸的价值主张不仅仅是"卖咖啡"，而是"通过数字化手段提供买得起、买得到、品质稳定的咖啡和茶饮体验"。这句话里的每个关键词——"数字化"、"买得起"、"买得到"、"稳定"——都在画布的其他模块中有所体现。比如"数字化"体现在渠道（App 和小程序）和客户关系（个性化优惠券）中；"买得起"体现在成本结构（需要严格控制原料和运营成本来支撑低定价）中；"买得到"体现在门店网络（高密度覆盖）和渠道（外卖配送）中。我们用 TikZ 画了一个标准的九宫格画布，让老师能直观地看到各个模块之间的位置关系。

**English:**
Next is the Business Model Canvas section. Our approach was to first define the Value Proposition, then expand outward to fill in the other building blocks. Luckin's value proposition isn't just "selling coffee" — it's "delivering affordable, accessible, and consistent coffee and tea experiences through digital technology." Every key word in this statement — "digital," "affordable," "accessible," "consistent" — is reflected in other building blocks. For example, "digital" appears in Channels (app and mini-program) and Customer Relationships (personalized coupons); "affordable" appears in the Cost Structure (needing strict control of raw material and operational costs to support low pricing); "accessible" appears in the store network (high-density coverage) and Channels (delivery). We used TikZ to draw a standard nine-block canvas so the teacher can visually see the positional relationships among all components.

## 10. BMC — 左侧：基础设施模块 | BMC — Left Side: Infrastructure Blocks

**中文：**
画布左侧的三个模块——关键合作伙伴（Key Partners）、关键活动（Key Activities）和关键资源（Key Resources）——描述的是瑞幸的"基础设施"。我们讨论时认为，瑞幸的 Key Partners 最有特色的是联营店运营商和外卖配送合作方——它们让瑞幸能以轻资产模式快速扩张。Key Activities 中，我们把"数字化下单和营销"列为第一项，因为这是瑞幸区别于传统咖啡连锁的核心活动。Key Resources 中，我们特别强调了"品牌和客户数据"——瑞幸在经历财务事件后能重建并快速增长，很大程度上依赖它在用户数据和品牌认知上的积累。这三个模块合在一起，回答了一个问题：瑞幸需要什么资源和伙伴、做什么活动，才能交付它的价值主张？

**English:**
The three blocks on the left side — Key Partners, Key Activities, and Key Resources — describe Luckin's "infrastructure." In our discussion, we thought the most distinctive Key Partners are partnership-store operators and delivery partners — they allow Luckin to expand rapidly with an asset-light model. Under Key Activities, we listed "digital ordering and marketing" first, because this is the core activity that differentiates Luckin from traditional coffee chains. Under Key Resources, we specifically emphasized "brand and customer data" — Luckin's ability to restructure and grow rapidly after its financial incident largely depends on the user data and brand recognition it accumulated. Together, these three blocks answer the question: what resources, partners, and activities does Luckin need to deliver its value proposition?

## 11. BMC — 右侧：客户界面模块 | BMC — Right Side: Customer Interface Blocks

**中文：**
画布右侧的三个模块——客户细分（Customer Segments）、客户关系（Customer Relationships）和渠道（Channels）——描述的是瑞幸如何接触和服务客户。客户细分方面，我们识别了五个群体：上班族和通勤者、大学生和年轻城市消费者、价格敏感型大众咖啡消费者、企业客户，以及偏好外卖或快速自提的消费者。我们讨论时注意到，这些群体有重叠但需求侧重不同：上班族更在意速度和地理位置，学生更在意价格和促销。客户关系方面，瑞幸最大的特点是 App 驱动的自动化关系管理——不是靠店员和顾客聊天，而是靠算法推送个性化优惠券和促销信息。渠道方面，App 和小程序是核心渠道，门店既是服务终端也是品牌触点。

**English:**
The three blocks on the right side — Customer Segments, Customer Relationships, and Channels — describe how Luckin reaches and serves its customers. For Customer Segments, we identified five groups: office workers and commuters, university students and young urban consumers, price-sensitive mass-market coffee drinkers, corporate and institutional buyers, and consumers who prefer delivery or quick pickup. During discussion, we noted that these groups overlap but have different priorities: office workers care more about speed and location, while students care more about price and promotions. For Customer Relationships, Luckin's biggest feature is app-driven automated relationship management — not relying on staff chatting with customers, but using algorithms to push personalized coupons and promotions. For Channels, the app and mini-program are the core channels, while stores serve as both service terminals and brand touchpoints.

## 12. BMC — 底部：财务模块 | BMC — Bottom: Financial Blocks

**中文：**
画布底部的两个模块——成本结构（Cost Structure）和收入来源（Revenue Streams）——展示的是瑞幸的财务逻辑。成本结构方面，我们把原材料、租金、人力、配送、营销、技术开发、设备折旧和加盟支持都列了出来。收入来源方面，最主要的当然是自营店的现制饮品和食品销售，但联营店的物料设备销售、加盟服务费和利润分成也越来越重要——这体现了瑞幸从纯自营向"自营+联营"混合模式的转型。我们还专门画了一张 FY2025 的收入结构图和一张成本压力图，让财务逻辑更加直观。我们讨论时的一个关键洞察是：瑞幸的收入和成本之间存在一种"张力"——低价策略驱动收入增长，但同时压缩毛利空间，这就要求内部流程和供应链管理必须极度高效。

**English:**
The two blocks at the bottom — Cost Structure and Revenue Streams — present Luckin's financial logic. For Cost Structure, we listed raw materials, rent, labor, delivery, marketing, technology development, equipment depreciation, and franchise support. For Revenue Streams, the primary source is of course fresh beverage and food sales from self-operated stores, but partnership-store material and equipment sales, franchise service fees, and profit sharing are becoming increasingly important — reflecting Luckin's transition from a purely self-operated model toward a hybrid "self-operated plus partnership" model. We also created a FY2025 revenue structure chart and a cost pressure chart to make the financial logic more visual. A key insight from our discussion was the "tension" between revenue and cost — the low-price strategy drives revenue growth but simultaneously compresses gross margins, which requires extreme efficiency in internal processes and supply chain management.

## 13. 两个框架之间的关系 | The Relationship Between BSC and BMC

**中文：**
我们讨论时还特别思考了一个问题：平衡计分卡和商业模式画布之间是什么关系？我们的理解是，BMC 告诉你"这个商业模式由哪些模块构成、每个模块的内容是什么"，它是一个静态的结构描述。而 BSC 告诉你"这个商业模式要成功，每个层面需要实现什么目标、用什么指标来跟踪"，它是一个动态的绩效管理体系。两者是互补的。举个例子，BMC 告诉我们瑞幸的核心活动包括"数字化下单和营销"，而 BSC 则进一步追问：这个活动做得好不好？用"App 交易成功率"和"订单路由错误率"来衡量。BMC 说成本结构有"营销促销支出"，BSC 则要求跟踪这些支出是否有效转化为"月均交易客户数"和"复购率"。所以我们在报告最后总结，这两个框架一起为后续的企业架构建模和数据库设计提供了基础。

**English:**
During our discussion, we also thought about an important question: what is the relationship between the Balanced Scorecard and the Business Model Canvas? Our understanding is that the BMC tells you "what building blocks make up this business model and what goes in each block" — it's a static structural description. The BSC, on the other hand, tells you "what goals each layer needs to achieve and what indicators to track" for this business model to succeed — it's a dynamic performance management system. The two are complementary. For example, the BMC tells us that Luckin's key activities include "digital ordering and marketing," while the BSC further asks: is this activity performing well? Use "app transaction success rate" and "order routing error rate" to measure it. The BMC says the cost structure includes "marketing and promotional spending," while the BSC requires tracking whether this spending effectively converts into "monthly transacting customers" and "repeat purchase rate." That's why we concluded in the report that together, these two frameworks provide a foundation for subsequent enterprise architecture modeling and database design.

## 14. 数据来源说明 | A Note on Data Sources

**中文：**
关于数据，我们在报告中引用了瑞幸 2025 年年度报告（Form 20-F）和季度业绩公告中的部分数据，用来支撑收入结构图和一些关键事实。但大部分 Measures 和成本压力评估采用的是定性判断而非精确数字。这符合老师的要求——这是一个课程案例分析，不需要大量精确数据，合理估算和描述性指标是可以接受的。我们在讨论时也尽量让指标具有"可操作性"：即使现在没有具体数字，也能清楚地知道如果要做实际评估，应该去收集什么样的数据。

**English:**
Regarding data, we cited some figures from Luckin's 2025 Annual Report (Form 20-F) and quarterly results announcements to support the revenue structure chart and some key facts. However, most Measures and the cost pressure assessment use qualitative judgments rather than precise numbers. This is consistent with the teacher's requirements — this is a course case analysis, so we don't need large amounts of precise data; reasonable estimates and descriptive indicators are acceptable.
